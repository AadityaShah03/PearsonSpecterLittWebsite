Our Retainer

At Pearson Specter Litt, we believe in providing our clients with transparent and cost-effective legal solutions. That's why we offer all of our services under a retainer fee structure, ensuring clarity and predictability in your legal expenses. With our retainer model, you gain access to a wide range of legal services tailored to your specific needs, all covered under one comprehensive fee. Whether you require assistance with contract negotiations, dispute resolution, or strategic legal advice, our team is here to support you every step of the way.

Our services are billed hourly, with a minimum commitment of 1000 hours. This approach allows us to dedicate the time and resources necessary to fully understand your unique circumstances and provide you with the highest quality representation. By billing on an hourly basis, we ensure that you only pay for the time spent on your matter, without any hidden fees or surprises. Our goal is to empower you with the legal guidance and support you need to achieve your objectives, while offering the peace of mind that comes with knowing your legal expenses are both reasonable and predictable. Trust Pearson Specter Litt to be your partner in navigating the complexities of the legal landscape, with transparency, integrity, and dedication at every turn.
